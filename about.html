Briana Haynes

Salt Lake City-based fashion and portrait phtographer who likes to spend much of her time on the road.

Capturing emotions is one of the most important parts of my work. I feel strongly about making the photographs that I can connect with. Even though it is the emotions of my subjects that I’m documenting, I find a reflection of myself in their world.

Think we could create something together? Feel free to reach out to me.
